# Bills Manager - User Guide

## Overview
The new Bills Manager provides complete management of all your recurring and one-time bills in one intuitive interface.

---

## Getting Started

### Access Bills Manager
1. Open FinanceOS
2. Tap the **Bills** tab (📅 Calendar icon) in the bottom navigation
3. You'll see all your bills with a summary at the top

---

## Key Features

### 1. Summary Cards (Top of Screen)

**Unpaid Bills Card**
- Shows total count of unpaid bills
- Updates instantly as you mark bills as paid

**Total Due Card** (Red highlight)
- Shows sum of all unpaid bill amounts
- Helps you understand cash flow needs
- Color-coded to stand out

---

### 2. Search & Filter

**Search by Title**
- Type in the search box to find bills
- Searches across bill names
- Real-time filtering

**Filter Buttons**
- **All**: Show every bill (paid and unpaid)
- **Unpaid**: Show only bills waiting to be paid
- **Paid**: Show only paid bills (for reference)

---

### 3. Add a New Bill

#### Option 1: Tap "Add New Bill" Button
1. Click the **"+ Add New Bill"** button at the bottom
2. The form will appear with these fields:

**Form Fields**:
- **Bill Title**: Name of the bill (e.g., "Netflix Subscription")
- **Amount**: Cost of the bill (e.g., 15.99)
- **Due Date**: When the bill is due (calendar picker)
- **Category**: Type of bill:
  - 💡 Utilities (electricity, water, gas)
  - 📱 Subscription (Netflix, Spotify, etc.)
  - 🛡️ Insurance (auto, health, home)
  - 🏠 Rent (housing payments)
  - 🏥 Healthcare (medical bills)
  - 🚗 Transport (car payments, gas)
  - 📋 Other (miscellaneous)
- **Recurring Bill Checkbox**: Check if this bill repeats monthly/yearly

3. Click **✓ Save Bill**

#### Validation
- Title and Amount are required
- Amount must be greater than 0
- Error messages appear if fields are missing

---

### 4. View Bills List

**What You See**:
- Bill emoji icon (based on category)
- Bill title (with strikethrough if paid)
- Category name
- "Recurring" badge (if applicable)
- Due date (e.g., "Jul 15")
- Amount on the right

**Color Coding**:
- **Green background**: Bill is marked as paid ✓
- **Gray background**: Bill is unpaid (needs action)

---

### 5. Edit an Existing Bill

1. Find the bill in the list
2. **Hover or swipe** to reveal action buttons
3. Click the **✏️ Edit** button (pencil icon)
4. The form will open with the bill's current information
5. Make your changes
6. Click **✓ Save Bill** to update

**Note**: Edited bills replace the original - no duplicate created

---

### 6. Mark Bill as Paid

**Two Ways**:

**Method 1: Click the Check Circle**
1. Find the bill in the list
2. Hover to reveal the **✓ Check Circle** button
3. Click it to mark as paid
4. Bill will turn green with strikethrough text
5. Click again to mark as unpaid

**Method 2: Edit Form**
1. Open edit form for the bill
2. Find the "This is a recurring bill" checkbox area
3. (Optional) You can also toggle paid status when editing

---

### 7. Delete a Bill

1. Find the bill in the list
2. Hover to reveal the **🗑️ Trash** button
3. Click it
4. **Confirm deletion** in the popup
5. Bill is permanently removed

**Warning**: This action cannot be undone. Confirm before deleting important bills.

## Tips & Best Practices

### 1. Mark Bills as Paid When Paid
- Keep status updated for accurate cash tracking
- Helps you see what's left to pay
- Prevents paying the same bill twice

### 2. Use Due Dates Accurately
- Bills sorted by date (earliest first)
- Know what's due soon
- Plan cash flow better

### 3. Set Recurring Correctly
- Check only for monthly/yearly bills
- One-time bills: leave unchecked
- Helps distinguish temporary from ongoing expenses

### 4. Choose Right Category
- Categories have different icons for quick scanning
- Categories appear in spending reports
- Helps with budget analysis

### 5. Search Before Adding
- Avoid duplicate bills
- Find existing bills quickly
- Update instead of adding new ones

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I set bills for specific dates (not monthly)?**
A: Yes! Just check the date and leave "Recurring" unchecked.

**Q: What happens if I don't mark a bill as paid?**
A: It stays in the "Unpaid" list and keeps counting toward "Total Due". Mark it when actually paid.

**Q: Can I edit a bill after marking it paid?**
A: Yes! Just tap Edit, make changes, and save. You can also toggle paid status.

**Q: Do recurring bills auto-repeat in the system?**
A: Currently, you see the bill each month until you delete it. Future versions may auto-generate next month's bill.

**Q: How many bills can I track?**
A: Unlimited! The system stores all bills in your device's local database.

**Q: Is my data backed up?**
A: Bills are stored locally. Backup your data through Settings → Export.

**Q: Can I see bills history?**
A: Currently shows only active bills. Historical paid bills remain visible if not deleted.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem**: Bill not appearing after adding
- Solution: Check search filters - make sure "All" is selected
- Solution: Refresh the page or navigate away and back

**Problem**: Can't delete a bill
- Solution: Make sure you confirmed the deletion dialog
- Solution: Try again - sometimes requires double-click on mobile

**Problem**: Edit changes not saving
- Solution: Check validation - all fields must be filled
- Solution: Make sure amount is greater than 0

**Problem**: Paid status not toggling
- Solution: Hover/swipe to reveal the check circle button
- Solution: Try clicking directly on the circle icon

**Problem**: Total Due amount seems wrong
- Solution: Make sure all bills are correctly marked as paid/unpaid
- Solution: Check filter - might be showing only unpaid bills
